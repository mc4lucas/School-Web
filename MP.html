Music Production
Music is quite often a major component in a game. This means that it is very important to have a soundtrack. There are many different sound production software applications, some are free and some are not. Just like everything else when it comes to game design, these applications have their perks and down sides.
Bosca Ceoil
This is a fairly popular soundtrack design software in the Indie game dev community as it is easy to use and can make some quality content. It is also free which is a perk.
If you are looking to make a small simple soundtrack then Bosca Ceoil may be for you. It is very straight forward, just select the instrument type and color in the boxes. Then just sit back in the glory of your hopefully amazing soundtrack.
Waveform Pro
Zynewave Podium
These are some other good applications that can be used if Bosca Ceoil is not for you, however keep in mind they are not free. Waveform does have a free "lite" version though.
