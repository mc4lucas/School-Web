Launching Your Game
There are many ways to make your game available to the internet. You can put it up in Steam as long as you have the $100 initial account fee and your ok with loosing a percentage of your revenue from the game. Another option is Epic Games. They do not charge an initial fee however they do take a percentage of your revenue from your game. If you are more interested in just making your game available and not worrying about the money, then itch.io may be the place for you. It gives your game a platform to be sold on and allows those who download it to donate what they feel is fair.
